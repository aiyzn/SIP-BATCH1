A Glimpse Into The Life of Others

Carmen's life took a tumultuous turn when she fell into drug use at a young age in her hometown barangay in Cagayan de Oro. Despite trying to escape the drug environment and achieving ten years of sobriety in Manila, she relapsed after returning to her hometown and marrying her second husband, who was also involved in drugs. In 2017, she was put on a watch list and given the opportunity to attend a Community-Based Drug Rehabilitation (CBDR) program as part of the Philippine Government's anti-drug campaign.


During the six-month CBDR sessions, Carmen learned valuable skills to manage her cravings and understand the impact of drugs on her life. With the support of USAID's RenewHealth Project, she successfully transformed her life, becoming a certified community facilitator and dedicating herself to helping others on their recovery journeys. Her journey, shared through the treatment booklet, became an inspiration for thousands across the Philippines, showcasing the possibility of positive change through CBDR. Carmen's story became a testament to the power of second chances and the belief that change is achievable, no matter the challenges faced.

Richard Elson's life had once been consumed by darkness. Trapped in the grip of addiction, he found himself spiraling into despair. It all began with a harmful encounter with Marijuana, or “Mary Jane” as he referred to it. Gambling and alcohol followed, pushing him further away from his loved ones and costing him his job. As the weight of his actions pressed upon him, he felt utterly worthless.

However, even in the darkest of times, hope never abandons the human spirit. Richard's parents and closest friends refused to give up on him. Their unwavering love, prayers, and support led him to a place called Fazenda da Esperança, a beacon of light for those seeking redemption from the clutches of addiction. There, Richard embarked on a transformative journey, finding solace in daily Gospel reflections and the grace to seek forgiveness. It was within the walls of Fazenda da Esperança that he encountered another Mary - Mama Mary, a symbol of refuge and strength.

Today, Richard's life has become a testament to the power of hope and renewal. No longer defined by his past mistakes, he works as a seminarian, dedicating his life to serving others and sharing his story of redemption. His fellow Fazenda da Esperança graduate, Kathleen Meneses, also found a new life after 17 years of drug addiction. Both of them bear witness to the enduring truth that no one is ever discarded by Jesus. Through their experiences, they remind us all that, with hope and faith, it is possible to overcome any darkness and emerge into the light, where love, forgiveness, and the strength of Christ prevail over even the strongest of chains.

Romano Vasquez's journey is a poignant tale of rising from the heights of fame to the depths of addiction and homelessness. Despite early success as a teen idol and member of the boy group Quamo, financial constraints pushed him into the world of sexy films, marking the beginning of a downward spiral into drug addiction.

Sleeping under the roof of a drug pusher, he desperately tried to fill the void left by his faded stardom with substances, and his life took a bleak turn as he roamed the streets, becoming a "palaboy" in Manila, trying to escape the harsh reality he faced. However, amidst the darkness, a glimmer of hope appeared in the form of Lina Gacula, who extended her hand to help him. Despite the temporary relief and support she provided, Vasquez's addiction proved relentless, leading to his eviction from Gacula's household.

In 2001, Vasquez found a new ray of hope in Alma, who would become his wife. The love and responsibility of fatherhood inspired him to seek a way out of the grips of addiction. Determined to turn his life around, he embraced the opportunity to work in multi-level marketing and share his life story as a motivational speaker. His journey serves as a powerful reminder of the dangers of addiction and the tenacity required to break free from its hold. Romano Vasquez's story is one of resilience, hope, and the pursuit of a brighter future, ultimately finding redemption through the love of family and the strength to overcome his darkest moments.
