Snakes n' Cakes

Slither your way to success in life in this all-new version of Snake.

You, Drake, are a youthful snake, eager to experience all that life has to offer. Yet, you find yourself surrounded by delectable cakes, each with the potential to bring either joy or sorrow. The challenge lies in whether you can withstand the allure and resist the temptation they present.

Cakes n’ Mistakes

Yellow Cakes serve as food. Gives 1 point.

Eat 4 of them consecutively and an Orange Cake (medicine) will spawn. Gives 5 points.

Eating a Cake of different color will break the streak and reset the amount of Yellow Cakes spawned in the board.

Know what cake you are eating. Others give no points and have nasty effects.

Know what cake you are eating. Others give no points and have nasty effects.

Red Cake || speed up (stimulant)

Blue Cake || slow down (depressant)

Purple Cake || switch cake places (hallucinogen)

Green Cake || changes everything to green (psychedelic)

Eating certain cakes can negate the effects of other cakes:

Red negates Blue

Green negates Green
